			<h3>What are Medical Services?</h3>		
		<p>Depression feels like living in a dark place and not wanting to get out to live the life you want. But every stage of depression can be cured. It is important to stay strong and do not hesitate to seek help when required. It is good to remember that different treatment methods treat different types of depression. These options range from self-help and therapies to psychological and physical treatments.</p>		
			<h3>Types of Medical Services</h3>

			<h3><a href="#selfhelp">Self-help and change in lifestyle</a></h3>		
		<p>Self-help and lifestyle changes is an effective tool to help you deal with depression. There are wide range of methods for self-help and lifestyle change available. It is important to know about things that you can do for yourself to lift your depression faster and feel much better. This treatment is the best way to prevent depression from returning and can work alone or along with physical or psychological treatments.</p><p>The most common self-help and lifestyle practices used to treat depression are to <strong>Establish a friendly emotional relationship, Lifestyle changes, Mindset changes and Seek professional help</strong>. You can learn more about these methods in below sections.</p>		
															<img width="2560" height="1707" src="https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-scaled.jpg" alt="" loading="lazy" srcset="https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-scaled.jpg 2560w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-300x200.jpg 300w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-768x512.jpg 768w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-1536x1024.jpg 1536w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Psychological-treatment-2048x1365.jpg 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" />															
			<h3><a href="#psy">Psychological treatment</a></h3>		
		<p>Psychological treatments are also known as talk therapies. Talk therapy is an efficient treatment method where it focuses on your way of thinking and provide you with skills and insights to change your thinking habits. These treatments helps you break barriers and overcome your life challenges.</p><p>The most common psychological treatment methods used to treat depression are <strong>Cognitive behaviour therapy (CBT), Interpersonal psychotherapy (IPT), Mindfulness-based cognitive therapy, Positive psychology</strong>. Most of the times, combination of these methods are practiced to aid depression. It acts as an alternative to physical treatments like medicines or sometimes works along with them. You can learn more about these methods in below sections.</p>		
															<img width="2560" height="1707" src="https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-scaled.jpg" alt="" loading="lazy" srcset="https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-scaled.jpg 2560w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-300x200.jpg 300w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-768x512.jpg 768w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-1536x1024.jpg 1536w, https://evocation.ml/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Physical-treatment-2048x1365.jpg 2048w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" />															
			<h3><a href="#phy">Physical treatment</a></h3>		
		<p>Physical treatment methods are used to treat moderate and severe depression. Physical treatment methods are considered to be taken after diagnosis via an appropriate assessment. The need for physical treatment occurs in case of specific symptoms, suicidal thoughts, feelings such as guilt, hopelessnees, unworthiness.</p><p>Medications can help to relieve your severe symptoms happening because of chemical imbalance in brain but it is not the most effective method. You need to remember that therapies and lifestyle changes can guide you well for your speedy recovery from depression.Â  The most common physical treatment methods used to treat depression are <strong>Drug treatments (medicines), Electroconvulsive therapy (ECT), Repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation (rTMS)</strong>. You can learn more about these methods in below sections.</p>
